Top 5 Indonesian Restaurants in Southern California.

I was born and raised in Indonesia and I love authentic Indonesian food. To the point where I live to eat Indo food. My favorite dish have to be Nasi Padang (Rice with veggies, rendang, fried chicken, Padang sauce and more). Using this map, I want to share my top 5 Indonesian restaurants that I believe are almost identical to the flavors back home. I hope that you can try and fall in love with Indonesian food just like I have!

By Ericko A. Halim

  • Merry's House of Chicken
    • Ayam Goreng Kremes (Fried Chicken with Crunchy Bits)
  • Bakmi Parahyangan

  • Medan Kitchen

  • Java Bistro
    • Nasi Bungkus Padang (Padang's Wrapped Rice)
  • Borneo Kalimantan Cuisine
    • Mie Karet Hokkien (Hokkien Style Rubber Noodles)
